FAQ's
What is included in interior and exterior painting services?

Our painting services include prepping the surfaces, applying high-quality paints, and ensuring a smooth finish for both interiors and exteriors of your property.

How do you clean and repair decks?

We start by power washing the deck to remove dirt and debris, then assess any damage. If necessary, we perform repairs such as replacing rotting wood or fixing loose boards.

How long does window blinds installation take?

Installation usually takes about 1-2 hours, depending on the number of windows and the complexity of the blinds being installed.

Do you provide TV mounting services for all types of walls?

Yes, we can mount TVs on all wall types including drywall, brick, and concrete. We ensure that the TV is securely mounted for safety and optimal viewing angles.

How do you repair vinyl siding?

We replace damaged panels, ensure proper sealing to prevent water intrusion, and re-align the siding to maintain the aesthetic and functional integrity of your exterior.

What is the process for vinyl floor installation?

We begin by preparing the subfloor, ensuring it's clean and level. Afterward, we cut and install the vinyl planks or tiles, using adhesive or click-lock methods, and finish with proper sealing.

What does a general contractor do?

A general contractor manages all aspects of a construction project, including hiring subcontractors, obtaining permits, and overseeing the quality and progress of the work.

Do you provide services for electrical repairs?

Yes, we offer electrical services such as installing light fixtures, replacing outlets, and wiring fans to ensure your electrical systems are functioning safely.

What types of plumbing services do you offer?

We provide plumbing services for minor issues such as faucet replacements, leak repairs, and the installation of water heaters and dishwashers.
